FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you complete my order? ▼

We accept orders with deadlines as short as 24 hours. Rush orders are available at a +50% surcharge. The sooner you reach out, the better your chances of securing your preferred deadline.

Is my order kept confidential? ▼

Absolutely. Your identity, your instructions, and your completed work are 100% private. We never share, publish, or reuse any client work — ever. Confidentiality is one of our core commitments.

What if I'm not satisfied with the work? ▼

We offer 2 free revisions on every order. If you're still not satisfied after revisions, we will work with you until you are. Your satisfaction is our top priority.

How do I pay? ▼

We accept PayPal and Wise. For orders under $30, full payment is required upfront. For orders above $30, we use a 50/50 split — 50% to confirm your order and 50% upon delivery once you're satisfied.

Do you handle all academic subjects? ▼

Yes — we cover a wide range of subjects including English, Nursing & Healthcare, Business & Management, Psychology, Social Work, Political Science, and many more. Contact us if you're unsure about your subject.

Will my paper be plagiarism-free? ▼

Yes. Every paper is written from scratch based on your specific instructions. We never recycle or reuse content. A plagiarism report is available as an add-on for just $5.

Can I communicate directly during my order? ▼

Yes. We believe in direct, transparent communication. You can reach us via WhatsApp, live chat, or email at any point during your order. We keep you updated throughout the process.
