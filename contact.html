FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you complete my order? ▼

We accept orders with deadlines as short as 3 hours. Urgency surcharges apply: +30% for 13–24 hrs, +40% for 7–12 hrs, +50% for 3–6 hrs.

Is my order kept confidential? ▼

Absolutely. Your identity, your instructions, and your completed work are 100% private. We never share, publish, or reuse any client work — ever.

What if I'm not satisfied with the work? ▼

We offer 2 free revisions on every order. If you're still not satisfied after revisions, we will work with you until you are.

How do I pay? ▼

We accept PayPal and Wise. Orders under $30: full payment upfront. Orders above $30: 50% upfront, 50% on delivery.

Do you handle all academic subjects? ▼

Yes — Nursing & Healthcare, Business & Management, Psychology, Social Work, Political Science, English, and many more.

Will my paper be plagiarism-free? ▼

Yes. Every paper is written from scratch. Plagiarism & AI reports are included free on every order.

Can I communicate directly during my order? ▼

Yes. Reach us via WhatsApp, live chat, or email at any point. We keep you updated throughout the process.
